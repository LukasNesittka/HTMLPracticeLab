Zelda Fan Portal

The Legend of Zelda is a franchise developed by Nintendo Entertainment Systems

The first Legend of Zelda game was released in February 21, 1986.

Since then, the series has experienced incredible growth, and has one of the most dedicated fanbases of any video game. The most popular Zelda games include:

  1. Breath of the wild
  2. Twilight Princess
  3. Ocarina of Time
